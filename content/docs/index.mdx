# SearchAF Documentation

Welcome to SearchAF &mdash; a powerful answer engine for every question.  

## What is SearchAF?

SearchAF delivers the answer engine for modern products:

- **Answers, Not Just Search Reults**: Understand user intent, not just keywords, with AI-native technology.
- **Scalable Without Worry**: Built on Antfly's proven, distributed architecture to handle hyperscale growth.
- **Secure from Day One**: Full enterprise-grade security is built-in, not bolted-on.
- **Connects in Minutes**: Native support for Shopify, WooCommerce, or simple patterns for custom platforms.

## Quick Start

Get started with SearchAF in just five minutes:

1. **Setup** (1 minute): [Sign up](/signup), created your [Organization](/docs/organizations), and configure your first [Project](/docs/projects).
2. **Authenticate** (2 minutes): [OAuth](/docs/authentication#oauth-20-authentication) your app (for Shopify), or grab your [API keys](/docs/authentication#api-key-authentication) (for custom apps).
3. **Connect** (2 minutes): Add your e-commerce platform to your SearchAF [index](/indexes).
